Our Story

From a trading floor in lower Manhattan to a platform across 180 markets.

STACKLY was founded by a small group of former NYSE specialists who believed retail investors deserved the same execution quality as institutions. We started with a single matching engine and a promise: no rebates, no payment for order flow, no compromise.

Today, that promise still defines us. We route every order on best-execution principles, and we publish our routing statistics every quarter for anyone to audit.
