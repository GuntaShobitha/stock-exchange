Active Trader and Institutional plans include Reg-T margin and tiered options approval up to Level 4 spreads.

Yes, 4:00 AM to 8:00 PM ET extended sessions are available at no extra cost on all paid plans.

REST and WebSocket APIs are free for all funded accounts. FIX 4.4 is available on Institutional plans.
